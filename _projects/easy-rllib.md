EasyRLlib is a lightweight PyTorch codebase for studying and implementing reinforcement learning algorithms, with particular attention to safe reinforcement learning.

The project provides a clear foundation for experimentation with policy optimization and constraint-aware learning methods. It also supports the algorithmic exploration used across my autonomous-driving research projects.

## Focus

- Readable PyTorch implementations
- Reinforcement learning and safe RL algorithms
- Reusable components for autonomous-driving experiments
- Research-oriented evaluation and iteration
